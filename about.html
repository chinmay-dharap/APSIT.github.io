About
APSIT  Talent  Fest

APSIT TALENT FEST is an open festival to entire Thane city hosted by the students of APSIT. Its an amalgamation of cultural, arts, literature, technical and gaming events comprising of intellectual as well as fun and creative events with a variety of scope for all.

It's not just any event organised, but an open platform welcoming all with an opportunity to showcase their talent to the entire city. So join us and express your hidden gems of talent and also compete with the best.
